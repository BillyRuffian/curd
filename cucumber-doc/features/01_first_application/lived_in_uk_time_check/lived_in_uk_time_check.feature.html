Lived In Uk Time Check

As a customer wishing to apply for a provisional licence online
I want to confirm the time I have lived in the UK within the last 12 months
So that I do not waste my time applying if I am not eligible for a provisional licence

Scenario: AC-02 - User has lived in the UK for more than 6 of the last 12 months

And
I have accessed the "Have you lived in the UK for more than 185 days approximately 6 months within the last 12 months?" page
When
I select 'Yes' and proceed
Then
I am redirected to the "Have you lived outside of the UK in the last 12 months?" page

Scenario: AC-03 - User has not lived in the UK for more than 6 of the last 12 months

And
I have accessed the "Have you lived in the UK for more than 185 days approximately 6 months within the last 12 months?" page
When
I select 'No' and proceed
Then
I am redirected to the "You cannot apply for a Great Britain (GB) provisional licence" page

Scenario: AC-04 - User has not selected an option

And
I have accessed the "Have you lived in the UK for more than 185 days approximately 6 months within the last 12 months?" page
When
I do not select an option and then select continue
Then
the following error is present:
