Upload a photo of your signature

As a customer wishing to apply for a provisional licence online
I want to be able to upload a digital signature for my provisional licence
So that I can continue to complete my application online and avoid providing a physical signature

Scenario: AC-01 Display "Upload a photo of your signature" Content

When
I have accessed the "Upload a photo of your signature" page
Then
the correct content is present for "Upload a photo of your signature"

Scenario: AC-02, AC-03 User selects "choose photo" and upload signature image

And
I have accessed the "Upload a photo of your signature" page
When
I select and upload a signature photo
Then
I am redirected to the "Check your signature" page

Scenario: AC-04 Display "Upload a photo of your signature" Content when JavaScript is disabled

And
javascript is disabled
When
I have accessed the "Upload a photo of your signature" page
Then
the correct content is present for "Upload a photo of your signature"

Scenario: AC-05 User selects "Continue" without uploading signature

The original AC-05 from DL-88 mentions JavaScript being disabled. Unfortunately the linking from
the error message to an input doesn't work in Firefox where the no_js tests are run so that
has been removed from this test as part of DL-1543

And
I have accessed the "Upload a photo of your signature" page
When
I proceed
Then
the following error is present:
Attribute Error
Error Summary Provide a photo of your signature to continue
Error Message Provide a photo of your signature to continue
And
clicking on the error link will focus the cursor into the corresponding field:
Error text Field ID
Provide a photo of your signature to continue capture_signature_signature
