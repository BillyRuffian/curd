Your passport photo

As a customer wishing to apply for a provisional licence online
I want to be able to use my passport photo on my provisional licence if I choose to
So that I do not need to spend time providing a new photograph

Scenario: AC-2 User selects “Yes” and then selects “Continue” while they have a valid passport signature

Given
I have accessed the service on GOV.UK
And
I have accessed the "Confirm you want to use this photo from your UK passport on your driving licence?" page
When
I select 'Yes' and proceed
Then
I am presented with the "Do you want to use this signature from your passport on your driving licence?" page

Scenario: AC-01 Display - "Confirm you want to use this photo from your UK passport on your driving licence?" Content

Given
I have accessed the service on GOV.UK
And
I have accessed the "Confirm you want to use this photo from your UK passport on your driving licence?" page
When
the correct content is present for "Confirm you want to use this photo from your UK passport on your driving licence?"
Then
the user's passport photo will be displayed as a preview

Scenario: AC-4 User has not selected an option

Given
I have accessed the service on GOV.UK
And
I have accessed the "Confirm you want to use this photo from your UK passport on your driving licence?" page
When
I proceed
Then
the following error is present:
Attribute Error
Error Summary Select yes if you want to use this photo on your driving licence
Error Message Select yes if you want to use this photo on your driving licence
And
clicking on the error link will focus the cursor into the corresponding field:
Error text Field ID
Select yes if you want to use this photo on your driving licence passport_photo_decision

Scenario: AC-15k User clicks the back link on dropout page - Decline to use passport photo dropout

Given
I have accessed the service on GOV.UK
And
I have accessed the "Confirm you want to use this photo from your UK passport on your driving licence?" page
And
I select 'No' and proceed
And
I am presented with the "Sorry, you're unable to continue as this is a new service in development" page
When
I click the "Back" link
Then
I am redirected to the "You will now need to provide a photo and signature" page
And
my previous answer will be displayed
