Address not found

As a service designer
I want customers who cannot find their addresses to have tailored screens giving advice on the specific situation they are in
So that they can make the best possible choice in how to continue with their application

Scenario: AC-01, AC-04, AC-06 'Address not found' dropout page content when no results are found for current address

Note: Static page content is asserted at the end as it doesn't change
Dynamic values are asserted on each iteration of the data table

When
I have accessed the "Find your address" page
Then
entering the following address details takes me to the "Address not found" page and the address details are present
house number postcode
DT511PB
100 SA18EN
And
the correct content is present for dropout page "Address not found for current address"
And
the 'Redirect to existing service' button has a link to the DLO page

Scenario: AC-03 Redirect to the dropout page from the 'Select your address' page - Current address

And
I have accessed the "Select your address" page
When
I click the "My address is not on the list" link
Then
I am presented with the "Address not found" page
And
the correct content is present for dropout page "Address not found for current address"

Scenario: AC-05 User clicks on the 'You can try again' link after attempting to find their current address

And
I have accessed the "Find your address" page
And
I submit a valid postcode which has not been found
And
I am presented with the "Address not found" page
When
I click the "You can try again" link
Then
I am presented with the "Find your address" page
And
I click the "Back" link
And
I am presented with the "What is your date of birth?" page
