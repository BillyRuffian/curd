Shortened address

As a customer wishing to apply for a provisional licence online
when my address is too long to fit on a driving license
I need to see a screen displaying what my shortened address will look like

Scenario: AC-02 Display page content

Given
I have accessed the service on GOV.UK
And
I have accessed the "Find your address" page
And
I enter a long address postcode and click on "Find UK address"
And
I select the long address and continue with my application
When
I have accessed the "This address will be on your licence" page
Then
the correct content is present for "This address will be on your licence"

Scenario: AC-03 User with pip

Given
I have accessed the service on GOV.UK with the identity: "person under 17"
And
I have accessed the "Find your address" page
And
I enter a long address postcode and click on "Find UK address"
And
I select the long address and continue with my application
And
I have accessed the "This address will be on your licence" page
When
I select 'Yes' and proceed
Then
I am redirected to the "Are you currently claiming Personal Independence Payment (PIP)?" page
And
I click on Back
And
I am redirected to the "This address will be on your licence" page
