Welsh Addresses

As a customer living in Wales
I want to see the English and the Welsh versions of my address
So that I can pick the version which I prefer to use

Scenario: AC-01a Current address list format including welsh addresses

And
I have accessed the "Find your address" page
And
I enter a Welsh address postcode and continue
When
I am presented with the "Select your address" page
Then
the list of English and Welsh translated addresses are formatted correctly

Scenario: AC-01b Previous address list format including welsh addresses

And
I have accessed the "How long have you lived at this address?" page
And
I enter a time of less than three years and continue
And
I have accessed the "Find your address" page
And
I enter a Welsh address postcode and continue
When
I am presented with the "Select your address" page
Then
the list of English and Welsh translated addresses are formatted correctly

the list of English only addresses are formatted correctly

And
I have accessed the "Your details" page

the previously selected Welsh translated address is displayed on the page
