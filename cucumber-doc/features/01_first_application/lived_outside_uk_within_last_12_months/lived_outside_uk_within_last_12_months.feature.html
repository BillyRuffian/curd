Lived Outside UK Within Last 12 Months

As a customer wishing to apply for a provisional licence online
I want to provide information on the countries I have lived in within the past 12 months
So that I can provide the correct information for my provisional licence application

Scenario: AC-1 Display "Have you lived outside of the UK in the last 12 months?" Content

When
I have accessed the "Have you lived outside of the UK in the last 12 months?" page
Then
the correct content is present for "Have you lived outside of the UK in the last 12 months?"

And
I have accessed the "Have you lived outside of the UK in the last 12 months?" page
When
I proceed
Then
the following error is present:
