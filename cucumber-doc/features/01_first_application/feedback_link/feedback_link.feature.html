Feedback link

All pages within the service shall have a "feedback" link in the header and footer.
This story is to describe how the user accesses the feedback survey, what information the service will pass along with them, and how they return from it.

Scenario: AC-01 Feedback link enabled header

Given
I have accessed the service on GOV.UK
When
I navigate to and click on the feedback link in the header
Then
I am presented with the "Give feedback" page

Scenario: AC-01 - Feedback link enabled header - applicant portal

Given
I have accessed the applicant portal on GOV.UK
When
I navigate to and click on the feedback link in the header
Then
I am presented with the "Give feedback" page

Scenario: AC-01 - Feedback link not displayed - applicant portal

Given
I have accessed the applicant portal on GOV.UK
When
I navigate to and click on the feedback link in the header
Then
The feedback link no longer exists

Scenario: AC-03 - Feedback link on FAP success screen

The feedback link will redirect the user to the survey
There is no success screen with return to gov at the moment

Given
I have accessed the service on GOV.UK
And
I have accessed the "Confirm you want to use this photo from your UK passport on your driving licence?" page
And
I have chosen to use passport photo and signature
And
I have accessed the "Payment successful" page
When
I click the "Return to GOV.UK" link
Then
I am taken to the snap survey

Scenario: AC-05 - Back link redirects to previous page from any point

Given

And

And
I am presented with the "Give feedback" page

Scenario: AC-06 Error message displayed when no characters entered

Given
I have accessed the service on GOV.UK
And
I navigate to and click on the feedback link in the footer
And
I am presented with the "Give feedback" page
When
I click on Submit feedback
Then
the following error is present:
Attribute Error
Error Summary Enter feedback
Error Message Enter feedback
And
that field shall also be highlighted as an error
And
clicking on the error link will focus the cursor into the corresponding text area:
Error text Field ID
Enter feedback give_feedback_additional_comments

Scenario: AC-07 Error message displayed when too many characters entered

Given
I have accessed the service on GOV.UK
And
I navigate to and click on the feedback link in the footer
And
I am presented with the "Give feedback" page
And
I enter overlength feedback in the feedback field
When
I click on Submit feedback
Then
the following error is present:
Attribute Error
Error Summary Feedback must be 500 characters or fewer.
Error Message Feedback must be 500 characters or fewer. & You have 1 character too many
And
that field shall also be highlighted as an error
And
clicking on the error link will focus the cursor into the corresponding text area:
Error text Field ID
Feedback must be 500 characters or fewer. give_feedback_additional_comments
