Back flow from identity

This story describes the backward flow of screen within the First Application after Identity - what each Back link should direct to.

Scenario: AC-01 - Over than 17 - Can't go back after verify

If the applicant is older than 17 years old, they will not be able to
navigate back once going through identity.

UPDATE
From DL-1067 Back link no longer present on page immediately after Identity
Changed test to use browser back link instead

And
I have accessed the "Contact details" page
When
I navigate backwards in the browser
Then
I am redirected to the "You're unable to go to the previous page" page

Scenario: AC-02 - Under 17 - Back button to PIP screen

If the applicant is younger than 17 years old, they will be sent to the
PIP screen when clicking on the Back link

And
I have accessed the "Your details" page
And
I continue the application using an identity of a person under 17
And
I have accessed the "Contact details" page
When
I click the "Back" link
Then
I am redirected to the "Are you currently claiming Personal Independence Payment (PIP)?" page

Scenario: AC-03 - Organ register back button

Clicking on the back link on the Organ register screen will send the
applicant back to the Contact details screen

And
I have accessed the "Would you like to be on the NHS Organ Donor Register?" page
When
I click the "Back" link
Then
I am redirected to the "Contact details" page

Scenario: AC-04 - Organ donation back button

Clicking on the back link on the Organ Donor screen will send the
applicant back to the Organ register screen

And
I have accessed the "Organ Donation Optional" page
When
I click the "Back" link
Then
I am redirected to the "Would you like to be on the NHS Organ Donor Register?" page

Scenario: AC-05 - How to photo and signature back button - Choose to donate

Applicant - Chooses organs
Clicking on the back link on the How to photo and signature screen will send the
applicant back to the Organ Donation Optional screen

And
I have accessed the "Would you like to be on the NHS Organ Donor Register?" page
And
I select 'Yes' and proceed
And
I select random individual organs
And
I proceed
When
I click the "Back" link
Then
I am redirected to the "Organ Donation (optional)" page

Scenario: AC-06 - How to photo and signature back button - No donation

Applicant - No organ donation
Clicking on the back link on the How to photo and signature screen will send the
applicant back to the Organ Donation register screen

And
I have accessed the "Would you like to be on the NHS Organ Donor Register?" page
And
I select 'No' and proceed
When
I click the "Back" link
Then
I am redirected to the "Would you like to be on the NHS Organ Donor Register?" page

Scenario: AC-07 - Passport photo back button

Clicking on the back link on the Passport photo screen will send the
applicant back to the How to screen

And
I have accessed the "Confirm you want to use this photo from your UK passport on your driving licence?" page
When
I click the "Back" link
Then
I am redirected to the "You will now need to provide a photo and signature" page

Scenario: AC-08 - Signature photo back button

Clicking on the back link on the Passport signature screen will send the
applicant back to the Passport photo screen

And
I have accessed the "Do you want to use this signature from your passport on your driving licence?" page
When
I click the "Back" link
Then
I am redirected to the "Confirm you want to use this photo from your UK passport on your driving licence?" page

Scenario: AC-09 - Check your answers back button - Passport signature used

Clicking on the back link on the Check your answers screen will send the
applicant back to the Passport signature screen

And
I have accessed the "Do you want to use this signature from your passport on your driving licence?" page
And
I select 'Yes' and proceed
And
I have accessed the "Check your answers before sending your application" page
When
I click the "Back" link
Then
I am redirected to the "Do you want to use this signature from your passport on your driving licence?" page

Scenario: AC-10 - Check your answers back button - Manual signature upload valid

Clicking on the back link on the Check your answers screen will send the
applicant back to the Check your signature screen

And
I have accessed the "Do you want to use this signature from your passport on your driving licence?" page
And
I select 'No, I'll provide my own signature' and proceed
And
I proceed
And
I select and upload a signature photo
And
I select 'Yes' and proceed
When
I click the "Back" link
Then
I am redirected to the "Check your signature" page

Scenario: AC-11 - Payment back button

Clicking on the back link on the Enter payment details screen will send the
applicant back to the Check your answers screen

And
I have accessed the "Enter payment details" page
When
I click the "Back" link
Then
I am redirected to the "Check your answers before sending your application" page
