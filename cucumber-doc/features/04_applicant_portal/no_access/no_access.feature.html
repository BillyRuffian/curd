No Access

As a service designer
I want a customer who tries to access a page they should not be able to, to be shown a “No Access” screen
So that there is no way for them to provide unexpected data

Scenario: AC-02 Continue functionality for no access page in applicant portal

Given
an application exists
And
I have accessed the applicant portal on GOV.UK
And
I have accessed the "Your provisional licence application" page
And
I visit the applicant_portal/display_no_access_to_this_page page directly in the applicant portal flow
When
I proceed
Then
I am redirected to the "Your provisional licence application" page
