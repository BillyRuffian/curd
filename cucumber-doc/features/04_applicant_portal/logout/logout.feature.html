Logout

As a customer managing my provisional licence application online
I want to be able to log out once I am done managing my application
So that my application details are secured

Scenario: AC-04 - Applicant selects logout

The user gets to the 'Your provisional licence application' page,
click on the 'Logout' link and get redirected to a survey page.

When
I click the "Logout" link
Then
I am presented with the page with url "https://wh.snapsurveys.com/s.asp?k=160450401185"

Scenario: AC-01b - Applicant cannot browse back once logged out

The user has logged out and has been redirected to the survey page,
they then click the back button in their browser,
and they are redirected to the applicant portal landing page

And
I click the "Logout" link
When
I navigate backwards in the browser 1 times
Then
I am presented with the "Sign in" page with the path "/applicant_portal/sign_in"

Scenario: AC-X1 - Applicant cannot revisit the application URL once logged out

The user has logged out and has been redirected to the survey page,
they then revisit the application URL in their browser,
and they are redirected to the applicant portal landing page

And
I capture the URL of the page I am on
And
I click the "Logout" link
When
I navigate back to the URL I previously captured
Then
I am presented with the "Sign in" page with the path "/applicant_portal/sign_in"
