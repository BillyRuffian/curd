Verify with both postcodes

As an applicant
I need to verify my identity using current or previous postcode
So that I can access my application

Scenario: AC-01a - Match old postcode

The applicant has a current address and a previous address in their address history
A previous postcode is that from the applicant's previous address

Given
an application exists
And
I have accessed the applicant portal on GOV.UK
And
I have accessed the "Find your address" page
And
I submit the previous postcode
And
I choose my previous address and time lived there
When
I complete verification
Then
I am redirected to the "Your provisional licence application" page
