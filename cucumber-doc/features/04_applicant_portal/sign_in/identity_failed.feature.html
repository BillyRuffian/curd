Identity Failed

As a customer
I want to know when I did not verify my identity
So that I can attempt to verify using the correct details and access my application

Scenario: AC-01 Sign in and verify using an identity that cannot be verified

When
I am presented with the "We have been unable to verify your identity" page
Then
the correct content is present for "We have been unable to verify your identity"

Scenario: AC-02, Applicant portal failed verification, session cleared

When
I am presented with the "We have been unable to verify your identity" page
Then
the applicant portal session is destroyed

Scenario: AC-03 Applicant portal failed verification and user opts to try again

And
I am presented with the "We have been unable to verify your identity" page
When
I click on Try again
Then
I am presented with the "Sign in" page
And
the "customer ref num" field is empty

Scenario: AC-04 Clicking the return to GOV.UK link

And
I am presented with the "We have been unable to verify your identity" page
When
I click the "Return to GOV.UK" link
Then
I am presented with the page with url "https://www.gov.uk/"

Scenario: AC-05e Continue journey from dropout page - After identity fail: Browser back button

And
I am presented with the "We have been unable to verify your identity" page
When
I navigate backwards in the browser 3 times
Then
I am redirected to the "Sign in" page
