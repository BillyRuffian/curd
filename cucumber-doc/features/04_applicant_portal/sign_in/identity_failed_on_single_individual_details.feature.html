Identity Failed On Single Individual Details

As a customer
I want to know when I did not verify my identity
So that I can attempt to verify using the correct details and access my application

Scenario: AC-05a - Cannot sign in with a first name that does not match my identity

As a customer
If I attempt to sign into the applicant portal with my first name spelled incorrectly
I expect to be blocked from signing in to the portal

Given
an application exists
And
I have accessed the applicant portal on GOV.UK
And
I have accessed the "Your Details" page
When
I try to sign in using identity details that have a first name that does not match the first name on my application
Then
I am presented with the "We have been unable to verify your identity" page
