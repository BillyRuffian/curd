Prevent Upload New Signature

As a service designer
I want a customer who is not expected to upload a new signature to be prevented from accessing the signature upload flow
So that there is no way for them to provide unexpected data

Scenario: AC-02-a Prevent signature upload when signature is pending review.

And
I have accessed the applicant portal on GOV.UK
And
I have accessed the "Your provisional licence application" page
When
I visit the applicant_portal/signature_capture page directly in the applicant portal flow
Then
I am presented with the "You're unable to go to this page" page
