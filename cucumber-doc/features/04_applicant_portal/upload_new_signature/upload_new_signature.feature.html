Upload New Signature

As a customer managing my provisional licence application online
I want to be able to upload a new image of my signature if it is rejected
So that I can continue with my application

Scenario: AC-01 - Clicking the continue button on the 'Signature Guidance' page takes the user to the 'Upload Your Signature' page

The user gets to the 'Signature Guidance' page,
they elect to continue, and
they are then redirected to the 'Upload Your Signature' page

And
I have started the invalid signature replacement journey
When
I proceed
Then
I am presented with the "Upload a photo of your signature" page with the path "/applicant_portal_signature_capture/capture_signature"

Scenario: AC-02 - Uploading an invalid signature image takes the user to the 'You need another signature' page

The user gets to the 'Upload Your Signature' page,
they upload an invalid signature,
they are then redirected to the 'You need another signature' page

When
I upload a new blank signature
Then
I am presented with the "Upload a photo of your signature" page
And
an error message shall be displayed stating "Your signature is not clear. Please ensure it is in black ink on a white background"

Scenario: AC-03 - Uploading a valid signature image takes the user to the 'Check Your Signature - Pass' page

The user gets to the 'Upload Your Signature' page,
they upload a valid signature,
they are then redirected to the 'Check Your Signature - Pass' page

When
I upload a new valid signature
Then
I am presented with the "Check your signature" page with the path "/applicant_portal_signature_capture/capture_signature_meets_rules"
And
an information message shall be displayed at the top of the rules screen stating "The photo of your signature has met the online checks. There will be internal checks to see if it meets our photo standards"

Scenario: AC-06b - If the user elects to use their verification-passed signature they are redirected to the applicant portal

The user has re-uploaded a valid signature,
they elect to continue with this valid signature,
they are then redirected to the applicant portal landing page

And
I upload a new valid signature
When
I select 'Yes' and proceed
Then
I am presented with the "Your provisional licence application" page with the path "/applicant_portal/dashboard"

Scenario: AC-07 - If the user elects to not use their verification-passed signature, they are redirected to the 'Signature Guidance' page

The user has re-uploaded a valid signature,
they elect to not continue with this valid signature,
they are then redirected to the 'Signature Guidance' page

And
I upload a new valid signature
When
I select 'No, I want to use another photo' and proceed
Then
I am presented with the "Upload a photo of your signature" page

Scenario: AC-08 - Clicking the back button on the 'Signature Guidance' page takes the user to the 'View Signature' page

The user gets to the 'Signature Guidance' page,
they click the 'Back' link on the page,
they are then redirected to the 'View Signature' page

And
I have started the invalid signature replacement journey
When
I click on Back
Then
I am presented with the "Your Signature" page with the path "/applicant_portal/view_signature"

Scenario: AC-09 - Clicking the back button on the 'Upload a photo of your signature' page takes the user to the 'Signature Guidance' page

The user gets to the 'Upload Your Signature' page,
they click the 'Back' link on the page,
they are then redirected to the 'Signature Guidance' page

And
I have started the invalid signature replacement journey
And
I proceed
When
I click on Back
Then
I am presented with the "Rules for driving licence signatures" page with the path "/applicant_portal_signature_capture/display_signature_rules"

Scenario: AC-11 - Clicking the back button on the 'Check Your Signature - Pass' page takes the user to the 'Signature Guidance' page

The user uploads a valid signature,
they click the 'Back' link on the page,
they are then redirected to the 'Signature Guidance' page

And
I upload a new valid signature
When
I click on Back
Then

The user uploads an invalid signature,
they click the 'Choose another signature' link on the page,
